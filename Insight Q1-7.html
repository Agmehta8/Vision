My Diagnosis

Patient Needs Assessment

Take a moment to reflect on what you need most right now. These seven prompts can help you capture the information that will guide your care conversations and next steps.

  1. Q1. What is the diagnosis, and what does it mean for me?

    Summarize what you know so far and any terms you want clarified.

  2. Q2. What questions do I need answered right away?

    List the most urgent questions you want to ask your care team.

  3. Q3. What symptoms or changes should I track?

    Note anything you are experiencing and what feels different.

  4. Q4. What are my immediate care priorities?

    Think about comfort, pain relief, or daily needs that matter most.

  5. Q5. What support do I want from my care team or loved ones?

    Identify who you want involved and how they can help.

  6. Q6. What decisions feel urgent or overwhelming?

    Capture any choices you are facing so you can revisit them later.

  7. Q7. What information or resources would help me feel more prepared?

    List what would make you feel steadier, informed, or reassured.
